Payment Instructions

Payment instructions will be provided following the March 13 planning meeting. Contact Lorenzo Slay, 2026 Reunion President, at lorenzo.slay@slaycorp.co with questions.

Travel Suggestions

The reunion takes place in Washington, DC from July 17-19, 2026.

  • The closest airports are Ronald Reagan Washington National (DCA) and Washington Dulles International (IAD).
  • Amtrak and regional trains arrive at Union Station, which connects to the Metro system.
  • The DC Metro is convenient for getting around the city. Consider a SmarTrip card for public transit.
  • Rideshare services (Uber, Lyft) are widely available throughout the DC area.

If you are driving, note that parking in downtown DC can be limited and expensive. Hotel parking or nearby garages are recommended.

Hotel Accommodation

Our reunion hotel is the Hilton Alexandria Old Town, located at 1767 King Street, Alexandria, VA 22314.

  • Dates: Friday, July 17 – Sunday, July 19, 2026
  • Room Rate: $169/night + 15.5% tax + $1.25/night facility fee (approximately $195/night all-in — this is an approximate estimate and not a guaranteed final total)
  • Parking: $29/night (discounted group rate)
  • Wi-Fi: Included

Book Within the Reunion Room Block

To receive the discounted group rate of $169/night, be sure to book your room within the Rowell Family Reunion room block. Booking details will be shared by email — contact Lorenzo Slay at lorenzo.slay@slaycorp.co if you need assistance.

Banquet pricing details are still being confirmed with the hotel. Final per-person costs will be posted here once available.

Saturday Museum Excursion

On Saturday morning, we will visit the National Museum of African American History & Culture in Washington, DC — approximately 7 miles from the hotel.

  • Admission: Free (timed-entry pass required)
  • Transportation: Charter bus from the hotel
Mark your calendar: Timed-entry passes for July release on Monday, May 18, 2026 at 9:00 AM ET. Passes go fast — be ready to book as soon as they open.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes for the museum visit
  • Formal or semi-formal attire for the Saturday evening banquet
  • Family photos or memorabilia to share
  • A light jacket or layers (museum and banquet venues may be air-conditioned)
  • Sunscreen and water bottle for any outdoor activities
  • Your confirmation details (check your email or the registration pages on this site)
