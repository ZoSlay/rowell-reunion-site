Lock the tumbler choice — Percy's Big Sur Grande is the working default at $5.39 each (50-unit minimum).

Lead time: tumblers usually ship in 2-3 weeks once art is approved. With the reunion on July 18, place the order by mid-June.

Bag assembly: 40 bags is roughly 1-2 hours of family assembly at the hotel Friday evening — could double as a hospitality activity.
5
Distribution: hand bags out at Friday check-in / welcome reception so attendees have the tumbler for Saturday's banquet.
