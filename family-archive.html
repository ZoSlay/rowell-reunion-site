Family Archive

A living repository for the Rowell family’s photographs, documents, and shared history

What belongs here

Photos

Reunion pictures, portraits, candid moments, scanned albums, and other family images worth preserving.

Historical documents

Programs, letters, family tree notes, church records, newspaper clippings, certificates, and scans of old papers.

Family announcements

Wedding announcements, birth announcements, graduation notices, memorial cards, and death announcements.

Theme-matching keepsakes

Anything that helps tell the family story for a reunion theme or special occasion — past, present, or future.

How it works: upload a file, add a title and context, and we’ll store it with the family archive. If the file is too large for the form, send the link by email instead. Please avoid anything private that the family should not share more widely.
